Up and Running
In 3 Steps.

01
Download & Extract

Download the ZIP file and extract it anywhere on your PC. No complex installation wizard.

02
Add Your Gemini API Key

Open Jarvis/app.py and paste your free Gemini API key. Takes 30 seconds — key is free from Google AI Studio.

GEMINI_API_KEY = "your-key-here"
03
Double-Click START_RAVEN.bat

That's it. Raven appears in the bottom-right corner of your screen. From now on it starts automatically with Windows.
